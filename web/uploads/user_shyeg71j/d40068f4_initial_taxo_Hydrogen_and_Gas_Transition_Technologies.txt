Label: Cost_effective_low_carbon_steelmaking_technologies
Dimension: Hydrogen_and_Gas_Transition_Technologies
Description: Hydrogen and gas transition technologies refer to approaches that inject hydrogen or hydrogen-rich gases into the blast furnace to partially substitute conventional carbon-based reducing agents, thereby reducing carbon emissions. This category includes injection facility modifications, gas composition optimization, and safety monitoring systems, enabling stepwise decarbonization within the existing blast furnace framework.
