Label: Cost_effective_low_carbon_steelmaking_technologies
Dimension: Fuel_and_Carbon_Source_Substitution_Technologies
Description: Fuel and Carbon Source Substitution Technologies refer to technologies that partially replace conventional coal- and coke-based carbon sources with low-carbon or renewable carbon sources in coke-making, sintering, and blast furnace processes in order to reduce fossil carbon consumption. These technologies aim to reduce direct carbon emissions while maintaining the conventional blast furnace ironmaking system.
